Posted in IES/Train Travel by TrainTraveller Alumni 2 days ago Tip

Booking Trenitalia vs Italo

For anyone planning train trips: always compare prices between Trenitalia (national railway) and Italo (private high-speed). Sometimes one is significantly cheaper or has better times for the same route. Use apps like Omio or Trainline to compare easily.
